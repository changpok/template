Background Information

The main vision for this industry is leadership, illustrated by understanding its guests and devising products and services that satisfy customer’s preferences and needs. Demonstration of care to clients is also a key value and is shown by attention to detail, remarkable excellence, timely services, warmth, concern and anticipation of customer’s needs.

The hotel workers, the owners and the tourists who are the clients mainly populate the hotel industry. To comprehensively carry out a research, this group of people would be very resourceful in analyzing the market trends and competency of staff, finding the key challenges to insufficiency and ways out to this problems gripping this sector and making future predictions of expansion and improved performance.

The chart below illustrates the composition of the hotel industry in almost every part of the world.From the above chart it is evident that the restaurant field is largest, followed by the lodging sector. Each of these fields consists of staff and management who oversee operations

Usage rate is the most fundamental variable in this industry, where hotel owners and operators of other fields wish that their services be on high demand in the market. The information about these services is exchange in traffic lines that vendors and consumers meet to trade. Like any other business, issue of competitive and absolute advantage issues an upper hand over your competitors. Quality and quick servicing is what defines the rate of demand in the industry.
