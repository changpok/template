Background Information

The main vision for this industry is leadership, illustrated by understanding its guests and devising products and services that satisfy customer’s preferences and needs. Demonstration of care to clients is also a key value and is shown by attention to detail, remarkable excellence, timely services, warmth, concern and anticipation of customer’s needs.

The hotel workers, the owners and the tourists who are the clients mainly populate the hotel industry. To comprehensively carry out a research, this group of people would be very resourceful in analyzing the market trends and competency of staff, finding the key challenges to insufficiency and ways out to this problems gripping this sector and making future predictions of expansion and improved performance.

The chart below illustrates the composition of the hotel industry in almost every part of the world.From the above chart it is evident that the restaurant field is largest, followed by the lodging sector. Each of these fields consists of staff and management who oversee operations

Usage rate is the most fundamental variable in this industry, where hotel owners and operators of other fields wish that their services be on high demand in the market. The information about these services is exchange in traffic lines that vendors and consumers meet to trade. Like any other business, issue of competitive and absolute advantage issues an upper hand over your competitors. Quality and quick servicing is what defines the rate of demand in the industry.

Apart from traditional classics like the location of the hotel and the support from people in the area, advanced technology, special services, themes adopted in restaurant, efficient and competent staff members have evolved as the new boosters and shaping elements of success in the hotel industry. Today, these hotels employ people of various educational background i.e. accountants, sales persons, marketers, public relations, chefs, cooks, security guards, receptionists, managers, human resource managers, IT specialists, communications specialists, Researchers from various fields etc.

Just like any other sector, research is very indispensible in the hotel industry. Through research, the challenges facing this industry can be uncovered and ways of dealing with them be devised by comparing the different strategies applied by each hotel. Research also exposes the performance of the hotels and the ranking of a hotel either locally or globally. Most hotels in Dubai are renowned internationally for their comfort and luxury, this serves as an eye opening to other hotels elsewhere. They will borrow some of the strategies used by these hotels in wooing their customers, for example provision of free wireless internet, transport to and from the airport and online bookings for rooms and conference rooms.

Research also helps in addressing key issues facing the human resource in a hotel. Research can divulge the best practices by a particular in a certain successful hotel which can then be applied to other staff through trainings and empowerments. Research also, bring ups new knowledge and technologies which can be applied to this industry to boost service delivery. The use of online communication in booking rooms and advertising services and products was an invention of the 21st century, which has greatly boosted service delivery by all means. All kind of information such as location, size, proximity to major beaches and oceans or parks, number of staff, services offered, room size among others are now available online in the hotel websites and social media pages like face book and twitter.

Research also focuses on ways of improving productivity through proper utilization of available resources to ensure efficiency and quality. Most hotels now embrace new and sophisticated ways i.e. use of ICT which helps to reduce the cost of advertising and transportation. Instead of hiring twenty sales people, for instance, hotels have switched to hiring one IT personnel who conduct adverting online and addresses all customer inquiries and complaints in real time. Research also aids when considering expansion to new areas or diversification of services and products.
