Introduction

The Hotel industry is a specialized field within the service industry that consists of lodging, event planning, parks, themes, and transportation, restaurants and cruise line among other field in the tourism industry (Lundberg, 204). It is a niche of business that strives to provide accommodations and food to the travelers. Travelers depend on hotels for temporary accommodation be it a family on vacation, couples on honeymoon, corporate business visit or purely tourism. Most hotels have comfortable rooms well equipped with basic necessities like closets, bathrooms, linens, beds, televisions, communication gadgets among others depending on the efficiency of the hotel and its target group of clients. Accomplishments in this industry are chiefly determined by good servicing to the needs of the client, creating a serene environment and diversifying services and amenities coupled with good communication skills.
